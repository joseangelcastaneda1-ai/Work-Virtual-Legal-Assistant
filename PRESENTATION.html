Document Analysis Process

Intelligent Document Classification

  • AI reads and analyzes uploaded PDFs and Word documents
  • Automatically identifies document types (passports, birth certificates, tax returns, etc.)
  • Determines document ownership (petitioner, beneficiary, applicant)
  • Extracts key information (names, dates, years, states, agencies)
  • Organizes documents into appropriate tabs (Tab A, B, C, D, E)

Smart Features

  • Detects translations and combines with originals
  • Identifies multiple letters of recommendation in single PDFs
  • Extracts tax years, driver's license states, and agency names
  • Prevents duplicate listings

VAWA Case Workflow

1. Upload Intake Form

AI extracts petitioner information, abuser details, and relationship data

2. Upload Declaration

AI analyzes abuse narrative and extracts cohabitation address

3. Upload Evidence Documents

AI classifies documents into Tabs A-E (ID, Status, Relationship, GMC, Other)

4. Generate Cover Letter

Creates comprehensive cover letter with organized document lists

5. Generate Legal Argument

Drafts legal argument explaining eligibility under VAWA

N-400 Naturalization Workflow

1. Upload Intake Form

2. Upload Evidence Documents

AI classifies documents into Tab A (Identity) and Tab B (Good Moral Character)

3. Document Analysis

Extracts specific details: tax years, driver's license states, letter authors, etc.

4. Generate Cover Letter

Creates cover letter with properly formatted document lists

5. Generate Legal Argument

Drafts argument explaining eligibility under INA Section 316
